The issue?

Clutter is profitable.

Your attention is a valuable commodity. It provides businesses with an avenue to promote their products, services or ideas. Today, capturing your attention has become a numbers game.

Some businesses still prioritise long-term relationships with their customers by having thoughtful interactions, pursuing innovation and delivering high-quality products or services.

But many won't. Especially in this industry, they've transitioned towards maximising short-term profit as their sole mission. This has come at the expense of quality, innovation, performance and passion.

So they sell their customers a product or service that lacks substance, personality or value. They sell clutter.


So here we are -

On a mission.

As an antidote to chaos, Simply Stellar emerged.

Our philosophy is embodied in our name. We want to prove that simplicity can be a powerful way to cut through the clutter whilst providing value and creating stellar and meaningful experiences.

Simply Stellar stands by the businesses we work with. Our business model operates contingent on the success of our clients. We offer our services at a low monthly cost that can be cancelled at anytime, no questions asked.

We've written more about our business model on our Pricing page.




What's our solution?

A solid foundation.

Our workflow process is down to a science and we're itching to talk to somebody about it.

We started by speaking to business owners to get insight on their experiences working with web-developers. Their experiences allowed us to identify common pain-points which we address in our creative and development process.

By putting our values into practice, we aim to create a better experience for you, your customers customers and raise the standard in your industry.
