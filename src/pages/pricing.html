Monthly Retainer

For newer and smaller businesses -

This option is perfect if you are a business owner that prefers to delegate the maintenance of your site to our developers. This plan includes unlimited minor edits to keep your site updated at no additional cost.

This plan is structured on a month-to-month basis, so you have the option to cancel at any time.



Full ownership

For growing and established businesses -

This plan is ideal if you have a team that can manage your site. During the development process, you'll be able to view your website in a test environment and provide input prior to completion.

Once completed, your team will be responsible for the management and maintenance of your website, however you can contact us for further technical support.
